How to make a grilled cheese:

  1. Get out sliced bread, cheese, butter, a pan, a plate, and lastly a knife.
  2. Heat butter and spread it across the pan
  3. Place sliced bread with cheese on top of one another
  4. Flip the sandwich over until it gets to your desired level of crispiness
  5. Place the sandwich on a plate and cut diagonally

How to play Rock Paper Scissors:

  1. You and your partner start by placing your fist into your palm.
  2. Then count to three and pound your fist into your palm on every count.
  3. After three say, shoot aloud, and on shoot, pick either a scissors hand position, a fist for a rock, or a resting hand for paper.
  4. Each partner will either have the same or a different position: scissors get broken by rock, paper covers rock, and scissors cut paper.
  5. Play three rounds, and the best out of those rounds wins the game!
